Edge Computing and 5G

Edge computing is a paradigm that brings computation and data storage closer to the location where it’s needed, rather than relying on a central server or cloud. This technology is critical for real-time applications and is made possible by the rise of 5G networks, which promise faster data speeds, low latency, and high reliability.
